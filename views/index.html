Fotoplastikon makes photo albums great again!

If you are here it means that you want to revive your traditional photo album. Fitoplastikon changes the expirience of famili photos watching by allowing you to add videos to your photo album. Isn't it great? To start follow few simple steps listed below

To create new album:

  • Login or if you don't have an account already Register
  • Go to Create Album overlap and create new album
  • Go to your album details and upload some videos
  • Generate QR codes (click the Get QR Codes button in album details)
  • Print and paste QR codes to your album

To view video from QR code:

  • You will need the smartphone and one of the QR code reader apps
  • Scan QR code
  • Go to the link
  • Watch the video!
